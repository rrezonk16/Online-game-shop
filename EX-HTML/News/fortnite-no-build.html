Fortnite brings no "ZERO BUILD" mode

Enes Paqarizi | Journalist Gamer News
ZERO BUILD IS A PURE TEST OF WEAPON, ITEM, AND TRAVERSAL SKILL - NO BUILDING REQUIRED. WITHOUT BUILDING, ALL PLAYERS HAVE THE RECHARGING OVERSHIELD AS THEIR FIRST LINE OF DEFENSE IN ZERO BUILD. SLIDE DOWNHILL TO AVOID ENEMY FIRE OR USE MANTLING TO GET THE HIGH GROUND ON OPPONENTS. DON'T FORGET TO SPRINT BETWEEN COVER ON YOUR WAY TO A VICTORY ROYALE!

What is Fortnite Zero Build?

Fortnite Zero Build is a fast-paced and more tactical version of Fortnite Battle Royale. This means players cannot build floors, ramps, or walls. If you played Battle Royale in the past and did not like building, then we got you covered! Zero Build gameplay requires utilizing your aiming skills and understanding your surroundings — to earn a Victory Royale, you will need to take cover and take aim.

How does Fortnite Zero Build change gameplay?

Fortnite Zero Build has all the same items and vehicles — and takes place on the same map — as Fortnite Battle Royale. Without building, players have a consistently recharging Overshield ability for an added layer of protection. New content additions and gameplay updates are often applied to both Fortnite Zero Build and Fortnite Battle Royale.
